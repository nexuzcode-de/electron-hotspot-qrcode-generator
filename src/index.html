    About

    QiFi generates WiFi QR codes completely locally. No network transmission is required. The application runs inside Electron and keeps your WiFi credentials on your machine.

    Saved networks are stored locally using HTML5 localStorage.

    WiFi QR format: WIFI:S:<SSID>;T:<WPA|WEP|nopass>;P:<password>;
